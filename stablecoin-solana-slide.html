Creator payouts are the wedge. Marketplaces are next.
Sources: Visa stablecoin strategy, Solana payments docs, Phantom Meta payout guide.
Stablecoins are moving from crypto trading into real payout infrastructure. The market already has scale: Visa estimates adjusted stablecoin volume above $10 trillion in 2025, with 316 million active stablecoin wallets. The reason this matters for creators is cost and speed. On Solana, a normal payment can settle for less than one tenth of one cent. Meta is the proof point: select creators can now receive USDC payouts, with Solana listed as one of the supported networks. My takeaway is simple: creator payouts are the wedge. Once platforms trust stablecoins for payouts, marketplaces and commerce are the next logical layer.
